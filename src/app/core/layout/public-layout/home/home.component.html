Everything You Need to Know at a Glance

With just one glance, you can track all your progress in the app over time. The dashboard provides information about your logged activities, completed ones, and total kilometers covered. There's no better way to stay motivated than seeing all your progress summarized.

Plan Your Next Goal

Think about the goal you want to achieve and how much time you think you’ll need to reach it. Don’t worry about how small it might be. Everything has a beginning, and every small effort counts. Little by little, you can increase your self-discipline and achieve the best version of yourself.

Order Leads to Success

All your activities at a glance. The goals table shows you the most important details of each one: the title, start and end dates, the total kilometers you've set, and the status of each goal.

In Detail

You can also view each goal in detail. The detailed view of each goal shows you all the data related to it. Additionally, it displays the total kilometers covered since you started the activity, as well as each individual activity you’ve logged. This way, you’ll have a detailed overview and a general idea of your progress. It will help you stay on track to achieve your goals.

Every Individual Effort Is Key

Each time you log an activity, simply enter the kilometers covered and the date. The app will remind you of your goal's date range as well as the total kilometers you set for yourself.
